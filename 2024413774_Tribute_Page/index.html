Ada Lovelace

The world's first computer programmer

Timeline of Ada's Life:

  • 1815 - Born in London to Lord Byron and Anne Isabella Milbanke.
  • 1833 - Met Charles Babbage and became interested in his Difference Engine.
  • 1843 - Translated Luigi Menabrea's memoir and added her own "Notes," including Method G—the first computer program.
  • 1852 - Died at the age of 36.
